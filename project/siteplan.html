Site Name

Photo Journal: An improved way of keeping a journal

Site Purpose

My wife and I always love to take pictures whenever and wherever we travel. The only problem is that after few months of taking photos, if we take a look back at some magnificent photos we've taken, sometimes we forgot the details of the event or what was memorable about the photo and where it was taken. For this reason, I am creating a website that will allow me to store photos with significant memories to it.

Scenarios

  • You can create a photo journal entry with brief description, date and photo.
  • You can dump any relevant photos you want.
  • You can store all your photo journals in the home page.
